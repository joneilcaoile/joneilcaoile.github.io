So I went to Stevens for my M.S. in Biomedical Engineering. Design verification, usability evaluation, regulatory strategy, the whole device lifecycle. I'm not here because AI is trending. I'm here because I spent two years watching the problem and couldn't stop thinking about it.

UseTrace HFE

AI-powered classification for medical device safety data

Problem Statement

Safety engineers manually classify hundreds of usability observations per study in Excel. It takes 2–4 weeks. Consistency across reviewers is low. There's no validated AI tool for it.

Design Solution

Takes study evidence (task lists, observer notes, device context) and classifies each observation with confidence scores and reasoning traces. The engineer validates a draft instead of starting from scratch.
